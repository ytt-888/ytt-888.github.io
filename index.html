What We Do

Engineering and Procurement

We don’t only sell you a product! We engineer it! We discuss with you, understand what you want, we present you the pros and cons, we offer you options and you choose. Then we calculate and draw, then we come back to you… We offer you a full set of documentation and we get it approved by authorities.

Installation and Commissioning

We supply and install systems for you… we test, commission and we start it up! Then we monitor it to prove that it works as promised. Our engineers are proficient in the latest software available: AutoCAD, Solid Works, PVSyst, PVSol, ETAP, Staad Pro, etc.

Service and Maintenance

We take care of our works, provide service and spare for its entire life of the system. We are not only in the office… but also on the field. Some of us have many years of experience on complex projects and we know what a client expect from us.

About Us

YTT SOLAR RESOURCES is a young company, set up based on Shams initiative of Dubai Electricity and Water Authority, which performs the full Engineering, Procurement, Construction and Maintenance (EPCM) scope in solar power systems We offer customized ON and OFF grid systems, solar/diesel hybrid systems, solar pumping stations, solar water heaters, street and ambient solar lighting.
Whatever is your requirement for a small house roof , a remote location OFF grid, an industrial roof top, or even a large scale ground mounted system we are here to offer you the best solution at a competitive price. We provide value added services throughout the life of the system, including operation and maintenance.
We are also providing custom engineering for automation projects, packaged / standard substations and LERs as per clients requests.

Services
